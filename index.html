01 · The swarm

Five organs. One unfakeable fitness function.

Population-based search with co-evolving evaluators is the 2026 frontier — and its known failure mode is drift: when the judge evolves too, what stops the population from gaming the judge? Here, nothing evolves its way around the referee: every fitness signal is anchored to kernel verdicts. Status of every organ: design — this page simulates the assembly and says so.

DESIGN
ORGAN 1 · POPULATION

Researcher Agents R₁…Rₙ

the conjecture swarm

Genome: heuristic profile + tactic portfolio + domain focus. Agents post and answer lemma requests on a shared blackboard — the verified DAG plus the failure ledger. Communication is stigmergic: every proof and every failure reshapes the terrain the others search.

DESIGN
ORGAN 2 · REPRODUCTION

Splice Engine

selection · crossover · mutation

Fitness = verified reach per unit compute — computed from build logs only. Top genomes recombine (portfolio ⊕ heuristics), mutants explore, an archive preserves stepping stones so the population can re-branch from any ancestor (the Darwin-Gödel-Machine lesson).

DESIGN
ORGAN 3 · JUDGMENT

Dynamic Evaluators E₁…Eₘ

the Red Queen, anchored

Evaluators triage what deserves prover compute: predicted provability, expected reach. They co-evolve with the population — but their own fitness is calibration against actual kernel verdicts (Brier score). An evaluator that drifts from ground truth dies. The Red Queen runs; the anchor holds.

DESIGN
ORGAN 4 · MEASUREMENT

Lean 4 Pipeline

the fixed referee

Unchanged and untouchable: candidates compile through lake build or they do not exist. Axiom audit on every acceptance — no sorry, no Classical.choice. The only organ that never evolves, because Löb says it must not judge itself (§03).

DESIGN
ORGAN 5 · MISSION

The Quest List

foundations of the ascent

The swarm is aimed, not aimless: formalize reflection steps, bounded-trust theorems, ordinal-measured strength growth — the open conjunction at the heart of RSI→ASI. AlphaProof-class systems hunt Erdős problems; this population hunts the mathematics of self-improvement itself.

The loop gain, population edition: a bigger verified DAG sharpens every agent's retrieval and every evaluator's calibration; better triage raises the kernel-success rate; every success feeds the DAG. Metric: reach — conjectures per epoch that fail under the previous DAG and compile under the current one. From build logs, nothing else.

FALSIFIER — if reach flatlines at 0 across epochs, the swarm is dead, and this page will say so.

03 · Constraints

Why the Red Queen cannot drift here.

Everything may evolve except two things: the referee, and the honesty of this page.

Löb invariance — the immutable referee

Blanket self-trust is inconsistent, not merely hard: T ⊢ □⌜φ⌝→φ forces T ⊢ φ (Löb 1955; Gödel II is the φ=⊥ corollary). So no organ of the swarm ever certifies itself — and the kernel is excluded from evolution by theorem, not by taste. Evaluators may get smarter forever; their verdicts are forever provisional next to the kernel's.

L0 · swarm output — proofs, procedures, genomes judged at L1
L1 · Lean 4 kernel fixed · external · non-evolving
L2 · kernel soundness assumed — stated, never claimed proved

The one fatal failure — a lineage that installs a false theorem — is structurally unreachable: everything enters through the gate or not at all.

Strict constructivism — total transparency

Every accepted proof ships its exact axiom footprint; the audit gate rejects Classical.choice, any sorry, any unjustified axiom.

Failures are first-class: counterexamples, kernel errors, audit rejections and culled genomes are published with the same fidelity as theorems. Scope, stated plainly: v1 improves capability at fixed logical strength — no ordinal moves until a machine-checked reflection step exists, and none is claimed.
