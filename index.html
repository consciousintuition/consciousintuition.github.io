01 · The problem

Self-improvement exists. A theory of it does not.

Three failures define the frontier — and all three are failures of missing mathematics, not missing compute.

P1 — GENERALIZATION

Learning does not transfer

Systems that master a distribution collapse outside it. There is no accepted mathematical account of when capability generalizes — the field measures generalization with the same benchmarks it trains against. A foundation that made "concept" and "transfer" into mathematical objects would turn an empirical mystery into a provable property.

P2 — UNVERIFIED RSI

The loop grades itself

The only self-improving systems that run validate their own rewrites empirically — the judge is a score the contestant can optimize against. Co-evolving evaluators sharpen the pressure but inherit the drift. Improvement without an ungameable referee is a claim, not a fact.

P3 — THE GAP ITSELF

RSI→ASI has no theory

The best available survey of the road to superintelligence names recursive improvement as a pathway and admits its dynamics are poorly understood — no precedent, no scaling law. Whether the loop compounds, saturates, or oscillates is exactly the kind of question only a mathematical theory answers. Nobody has one.

02 · The program

A swarm that proposes theories — judged constructively.

Not a theorem factory. The unit of output is a theory: definitions, axioms, structures in the landscape where foundations are actively being rebuilt — homotopy type theory and univalent foundations, ∞-toposes, synthetic and modal approaches. Machine verification is one organ among five, deliberately small: the kernel says sound, never interesting. Status of every organ: design. This page simulates the assembly and says so.

ORGAN 1 · PROPOSALDESIGN

Theory Proposers T₁…Tₙ

LLM agents over the foundations landscape

Each agent drafts candidate structures — a new modality, a descent condition, a universe principle, an invariant — stated in the internal language of univalent foundations. Agents share a blackboard of definitions, counterexamples and failed axioms; communication is the medium of the search.

ORGAN 2 · REPRODUCTIONDESIGN

Splice Engine

selection · crossover · mutation

Fitness is verified consequence per unit compute: a proposed theory earns fitness when its definitions support machine-checked, constructive results downstream. Top genomes recombine; an archive keeps stepping stones alive for re-branching.

ORGAN 3 · JUDGMENTDESIGN

Dynamic Evaluators E₁…Eₘ

the Red Queen, anchored

Evaluators triage: which proposals deserve formalization compute, which smell inconsistent, which are re-inventions. They co-evolve with the proposers — and cannot drift, because their own fitness is calibration against eventual kernel verdicts. A judge that departs from ground truth is culled.

ORGAN 4 · MEASUREMENTDESIGN

Intuitionistic Verification

small, fixed, final

Constructive proof kernels — cubical type checkers for the HoTT-native fragment, Lean 4 for classical-free elementary results — collapse |ψ⟩. This organ is deliberately the smallest: it never proposes, never ranks, never evolves. It only answers. Every acceptance ships its axiom footprint; nothing non-constructive passes.

ORGAN 5 · MISSIONDESIGN

The Quest

a theory for the gap

The swarm is aimed at the mathematics the gap is missing: a compositional account of generalization; reflection and bounded self-trust; strength that grows along measurable ordinals; invariants that predict when an improvement loop compounds. New foundations as instrument — the RSI→ASI gap as target.

The loop gain: every verified consequence enriches the blackboard every proposer and evaluator searches; better triage buys more verified consequences per epoch. Metric — reach: results per epoch that were unreachable under the previous library and are machine-checked under the current one. Computed from kernel logs, nothing else.

FALSIFIER — if reach flatlines at 0 across epochs, the swarm is dead, and this page will say so.

04 · Constraints

Everything evolves except the referee.

Two invariants, held by construction — the reason this program's claims can be strict where the field's are statistical.

Löb invariance — why the referee is frozen

Blanket self-trust is inconsistent, not merely hard: T ⊢ □⌜φ⌝→φ forces T ⊢ φ (Löb 1955; Gödel II is the φ=⊥ corollary). So the verification organ is excluded from evolution by theorem, not by taste — evaluators may sharpen forever; their verdicts stay provisional next to the kernel's.

L0 · swarm output — theories, proofs, genomes judged at L1
L1 · constructive kernels fixed · external · non-evolving
L2 · kernel soundness assumed — stated, never claimed proved

A lineage that installs a false result is structurally unreachable: everything enters through the gate or not at all.

Intuitionistic by construction

The native logic of the foundations we build on is constructive — a proof is an object, not a double negation. The audit gate enforces it: every acceptance ships its axiom footprint, and anything classical is rejected, logged, and returned as selection pressure.
